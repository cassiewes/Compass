Frequently Asked Questions

Can I select multiple activities?

Yes, you can select an unlimited number of activities! To do so, just click the icons associated with your desired activities. A grey border will appear to let you know that your selection was successfully submitted.

How do I get to the next page?

You must select at least one activity before you can continue to the next page!

What if I'm not sure what type of activity I'd like to do?

You can always select multiple, or all!

What if I change my mind

Coming back to this page is always possible

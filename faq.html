Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view the directions to a particular place?

To view directions, simply click on the location in the map OR click on the location in the results tab.

I don't like my results. How do I start from scratch?

Directly above the map, there is a link that will refresh your searches. Otherwise, you can also use the "back" button to refine your submissions.

Where can I find the rating for each location?

Click on the location in the map or click on the location in the results tab to view the rating.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pick a mode of transit?

Simply mouse over one of the three words (walk, drive, or transit) and click on it. That will bring you to the page where you can select activities.

How do I get to the next page?

You must select a mode of transit to get to the next page. Click one of the three options: walk, drive, or transit!

What if I change my mind?

You can always go back on the following pages!

What if I'm not sure what mode of transit I'll be using?

As we said, you can always go back! But also, the mode of transit is just used for calculating directions, so it won't affect the results if you end up choosing a different mode of transportation!
